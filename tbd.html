THE BLOG DUNGEON

March 13th, 2026 2:28AM

This is my first post! This is also my first attempt at building a website so this page and the others before it are very work in progress. My code is messy, but I'm learning a lot and excited to be part of the community! I'm the kind of person that loves an ongoing project next to all my day to day to bounce back and forth from. That being said, website development has been a fantastic distraction for me. I find it almost meditative to create through problem solving!

Like i said, this websites development is quite ongoing and I have lots of plans for it. I want to have a bookshelf to talk about what I'm reading, a game museum to show off my favorite games, and maybe something weird and interractive like a live feed to my 3D printer or perhaps a readout of what my fridge's temperature is at all times. There will also probably be a section where I write about my wild hyperfixations like Bionicle lore and copper as a material. This is all very normal and regular.
